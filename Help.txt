				Game instructions   
	Use arrow keys to move the snake.
		-> You will be provided foods at the several coordinates of the 
		screen which you have to eat. Everytime you eat a food the length 
		of the snake will be increased by 1 element and thus the score.
		-> Here you are provided with three lives. Your life will decrease 
		as you hit the wall or snake's body.
		-> YOu can pause the game in its middle by pressing any key. To 
		continue the paused game press any other key once again
		-> If you want to exit press esc.
